1 Secure onboarding with role selection and wallet creation
2 Register restoration projects with geotagged baselines
3 Offline-first data capture; auto-sync on connectivity
4 Periodic monitoring updates on biomass and health
5 Real-time verification requests and feedback
6 Credit portfolio with on-chain transactions

Web dashboard (admins & verifiers)

1 Role-based access for administrators and verifiers
2 Review/approve project proposals and submissions
3 Automated validation by cross-referencing sources
4 Digitally sign and anchor verified data on-chain

6 GIS dashboards, analytics, and audit trails
